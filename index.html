How to Play

1. Select hunting areas to start. Different grounds mean different levels.

2. Click the black monster icon to find out the hidden monster.

3. Each turn you can select 2 monsters. If the two monsters are the same, you defeat them and gain points.

4. If they are different monsters, you will get hurt and lose Health Point.

WIN: Find out all matching monsters and make as few mistakes as possible.

LOSE: Health Point reach 0%.
